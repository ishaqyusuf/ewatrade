## Question

What should the first inbound creation and review experience look like for supplier purchases and production batches?

Prototype enough to decide:

- Where the feature lives in navigation and settings relative to Products, Stock, Reports, and Billing.
- The form for supplier purchase lines, quantities, supplier costs, extra costs, landed cost totals, and markup.
- The form for production batch output quantity and lower-section cost lines.
- Draft, review, approve/post, and history screens.
- How users switch between current business context before creating an inbound.
- Mobile versus dashboard responsibility for v1.
- Empty, error, partial receipt, posted, and permission-denied states.

The artifact may be a markdown wireflow or low-fidelity UI sketch in this scratch directory.
