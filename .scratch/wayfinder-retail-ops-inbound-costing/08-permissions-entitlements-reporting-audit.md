## Question

What permission, subscription, reporting, and audit rules should apply to inbound costing?

Resolve at least:

- Which roles can create, edit, approve, cancel, post, and view cost details.
- Whether sales reps/cashiers can receive stock without seeing landed cost or profit margin.
- Whether cost price, landed cost, margin, and supplier information are owner/admin/manager only.
- Whether inbound count, production batch count, cost-history depth, or advanced allocation methods should be plan-limited.
- How per-business billing should display this feature in plan usage without crossing tenant boundaries.
- What audit trail is required for changes to costs, quantities, approval, posting, and selling price updates.
- What reports need inbound cost data: stock value, gross margin, landed cost variance, production cost, and supplier history.

The answer should produce API permission rules and reporting boundaries ready for Brain/API docs.
