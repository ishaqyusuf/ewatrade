## Question

What should the inbound workspace be called, and what lifecycle states should it support from costing draft to stock posting?

Resolve at least:

- Whether user-facing language is `Inbound`, `Stock order`, `Purchase batch`, `Production batch`, `Stock arrival`, or another term.
- Whether supplier purchases and production/manufacturing batches share the same lifecycle or need different wording.
- Draft, ordered, in transit, costed, partially received, received, approved, posted, cancelled, and reopened states.
- Who can edit quantities and costs at each state.
- When inventory stock is allowed to increase.
- What happens when expected quantity differs from received quantity.
- Whether an inbound can create new products/units, only update existing units, or both.

The answer should define the v1 lifecycle and the terms that should appear in UI/API docs.
