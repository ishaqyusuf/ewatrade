## Question

What gaps remain in the current multi-business and subscription implementation for a user who is invited into one business as a sales rep, but also wants to create, own, switch to, and pay for their own separate business?

Research at least:

- How dashboard tenant switching currently handles a user with memberships in multiple tenants and different roles.
- How mobile local and production session context currently resolves active business/store.
- Whether staff/cashier/operator roles can create their own tenant from the same account without gaining owner/admin access to the invited business.
- Whether business creation limits should apply to the tenant being created under, the user's current active tenant, or a future account-level policy.
- Whether subscription reads and entitlement checks already make each tenant/business independently billed.
- What needs to change in settings/profile UI to show current business, available businesses, invited roles, owned businesses, and create-business entry points without confusing `Tenant` and `Store`.

The answer should produce a gap list and recommended reuse points, not implementation code.
