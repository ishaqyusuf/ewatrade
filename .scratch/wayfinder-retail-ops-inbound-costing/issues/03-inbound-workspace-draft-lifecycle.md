# 03 - Inbound Workspace Draft Lifecycle

**What to build:** Owners and managers can open a dashboard-first Inbound workspace for the active business, create supplier-purchase or production-batch drafts, edit them, view detail, list existing inbounds, and cancel drafts without affecting stock.

**Blocked by:** 01 - Business Account Switch And Create

**Status:** ready-for-agent

- [ ] The Inbound workspace is scoped to the active tenant/store and shows the active business context.
- [ ] Users can create supplier-purchase and production-batch draft records.
- [ ] Users can edit draft identity fields, notes, source labels, and mode-specific setup fields.
- [ ] Users can list and open inbound records by status, type, date, and business/store context.
- [ ] Users can cancel draft inbound records.
- [ ] Draft and cancelled inbound records do not change inventory, prices, stock movements, or reports.
- [ ] Authorization tests prove users cannot access inbound records outside their tenant/store scope.
