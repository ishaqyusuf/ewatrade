# 01 — Owner Signup, OTP, And Business Entry

**What to build:** a new or returning owner can open the mobile app, sign up or log in with Google/Gmail or email OTP, verify by email, and land in the correct business context without entering a bulky form. This ticket should connect the minimal mobile auth screens to the production auth/session path and preserve the existing local fallback only where production is unavailable.

**Blocked by:** None — can start immediately.

**Status:** implementation-complete

- [x] The mobile login screen has a clear sign-up path and supports Google/Gmail plus email OTP entry.
- [x] Email signup collects only name, email address, and business name before OTP verification.
- [x] OTP verification creates or resumes the owner session and resolves the active business context.
- [x] Returning login supports Google/Gmail or email OTP without requiring a password for the MVP flow.
- [x] Session and tenant context are stored securely enough for offline use after first successful login.
- [x] Keyboard behavior is verified for login, signup, and OTP screens on compact phones.
