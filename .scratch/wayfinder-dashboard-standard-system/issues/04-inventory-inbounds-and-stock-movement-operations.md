# 04 - Inventory, Inbounds, And Stock Movement Operations

**What to build:** managers can review stock by product and unit, record inbound stock intake, record stock adjustments, perform unit conversions, and inspect stock movement history through the dashboard.

**Blocked by:** 03 - Products Proof Slice With Table, Sheet, And Form Foundations.

**Status:** ready-for-agent

- [ ] Inventory table shows product/unit stock, low-stock or out-of-stock state, and relevant filters.
- [ ] Stock intake can be recorded from the dashboard with clear validation and permission handling.
- [ ] Stock adjustments can be recorded with reason, direction, and optional notes where supported by the API.
- [ ] Unit conversion or rebagging can be recorded between supported product units.
- [ ] Stock movement history is visible with date, product/unit, movement type, actor, and source context where available.
- [ ] Browser QA covers inventory viewing, stock intake, stock adjustment, unit conversion, stock movement filtering, and role-gated access.
- [ ] API and Brain docs are updated if new dashboard-facing stock contracts or behavior are added.
