## Context

The user wants the same form system, side sheet, sheet and modal behavior, and registration approach as Midday. Candidate workflows include product creation/editing, inventory intake, staff invite, payroll actions, business switching, settings, shared-link follow-up, subscription checkout, and sync conflict review.

## Resolution Target

- Global sheet provider and global sheet registry shape.
- Modal provider and registration shape.
- Form validation, mutation, error display, loading, and invalidation conventions.
- Which workflows use sheets versus modals versus full pages.
- How destructive or high-risk actions are confirmed.
- What HalalVest patterns can be reused after Midday alignment.
