## Question

What analytics, reporting, chart, and operational data visualization patterns should the playground include for mobile Retail Ops?

Resolve KPI cards, mini stat rows, bar charts, report sections, legends, empty/loading/error chart states, chart color semantics, compact date/filter controls, and how analytics patterns stay readable on small phones in both light and dark mode.

## Comments

> *This was generated by AI during Wayfinder issue commenting.*

Proposed answer: Include KPI tiles, report metric tiles, bounded report rows, compact filters, a simple vertical bar chart, chart legend, and empty/loading/error chart states. Use Retail Ops sample data: sales today, cash/transfer split, low stock, staff sessions, share-link views/orders, sync conflicts, and inventory movement.

Why: Analytics must feel native to the operational app, not like a separate dashboard squeezed onto mobile.

Assumptions / risk: If no chart library is already accepted, first implementation should use a lightweight native/SVG bar chart or simple view-based bars before adding dependency weight.
