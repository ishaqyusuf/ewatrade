# 03 — Deliver advanced variants, Offerings, and Store availability

**What to build:** Let merchants define neutral Variant Option Groups, generate
and selectively enable combinations, configure independent Product or Service
Offerings, identifiers, prices, and Store availability without giving variants
unit or stock meaning.

**Blocked by:** 02 — Deliver simple Product and Service setup end to end

**Status:** source-complete-pending-behavioral-validation

- [ ] Merchant option groups and values generate stable unique Sellable Variants, including disabled combinations.
- [ ] Product and Service Offerings retain independent fixed or quote-required pricing where permitted.
- [ ] SKU and barcode uniqueness is enforced at the business-owned Product Unit Offering boundary.
- [ ] Store Offering Availability is independent from catalog activation and current stock.
- [ ] Used variants/Offerings are archived rather than deleted or retyped.
- [ ] Dashboard and mobile advanced setup prove colour/size and garment/size examples without special fields.
