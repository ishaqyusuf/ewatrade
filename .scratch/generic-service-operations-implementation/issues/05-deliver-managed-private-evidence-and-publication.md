# 05 — Deliver managed private Service Evidence and publication

**What to build:** Let authorized staff capture or upload purpose-labelled
photos, videos, and files against a Job or Job Line, keep them private by
default, recover failed uploads, and explicitly publish or revoke safe evidence.

**Blocked by:** 01 — Deliver direct Service Intake through Order and tracked work; 02 — Deliver Job Line progress and partial readiness

**Status:** source-complete-pending-behavioral-validation

- [ ] Evidence records target, purpose, media type, uploader, capture/upload times, asset reference, visibility, and upload lifecycle.
- [ ] Intake confirmation and unrelated work continue when evidence is absent, queued, or failed.
- [ ] Publication requires dedicated capability, available asset, valid customer scope, and explicit action.
- [ ] Private, local, failed, unavailable, or revoked assets never appear in public projections or leak storage identifiers.
- [ ] Revocation leaves an audited tombstone and access/publication/revocation events are reviewable without exposing contents.
- [ ] Mobile capture/retry and dashboard review/publication flows cover photos, videos, failure, and permission denial.
