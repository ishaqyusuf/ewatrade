# 01 - Business Template Foundation And Product Sales Compatibility

**What to build:** Ewatrade can resolve an effective business template for every tenant/store, default existing businesses safely to Product Sales, and keep current Product Sales/Retail Ops behavior unchanged while the template foundation exists beside it.

**Blocked by:** None - can start immediately.

**Status:** ready-for-agent

- [ ] Existing tenants/stores without an explicit template resolve to Product Sales by default.
- [ ] A new system-owned template registry exposes at least Product Sales, Dry Cleaning/Laundry, and Other Generic keys.
- [ ] Product Sales keeps existing inventory, products, variants, customers, sales reps, stock custody, share links, closeout, and reports behavior unchanged.
- [ ] The effective template is visible to authorized internal/support surfaces or debug output without exposing unsafe controls to ordinary staff.
- [ ] Automated tests prove Product Sales compatibility for representative onboarding, product, sale, customer, staff, and report behavior.
