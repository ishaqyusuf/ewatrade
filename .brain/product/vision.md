# Product Vision

## Purpose
Capture the product direction and who the platform is for.

## How To Use
- Update when target market, positioning, or core outcomes change.

## Vision
Build commerce and logistics infrastructure for African merchants that combines storefronts, merchant operations, fulfillment coordination, and communication workflows in one multi-tenant platform.

## Primary Users
- Merchants
- Business owners and managers running daily retail operations
- Dispatch providers
- Store cashiers and sales attendants
- End customers

## Differentiation
- Commerce plus logistics in one platform
- Strong multi-store and multi-tenant model
- Website builder and marketplace support
- Messaging and assisted checkout workflows
- Offline-first sales, inventory, rep sessions, and reconciliation for small retail teams

## Retail Ops Product Direction
- The mobile app is the first focused Retail Ops surface for owners and attendants.
- Owners need a simple path from sign-up to first product, first sale, staff invite, and closeout.
- Attendants need a fast clock-in, opening inventory, sale capture, customer capture, and offline sync workflow.
- Admin reporting should explain sales, payment totals, stock balances, rep activity, variances, share-link orders, and subscription state without becoming broad BI.
- Feed, bag, half-bag, and quarter-bag flows are starter templates for flexible-unit inventory businesses, not the full product boundary.

## Product Wedge
- Retail Ops/Sales is the first ewatrade wedge because it creates daily operational value before broader storefront, marketplace, logistics, and automation layers are fully mature.
- The wedge should prove merchant onboarding, product/unit modeling, stock ledger, staff workflow, offline sync, reconciliation, reports, and subscription packaging.
- See `.brain/features/retail-ops-sales-product.md` for the A-to-Z product direction.
